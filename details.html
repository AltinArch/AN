Technical Details

Every joint, node, and threshold in Altan Nexus is the result of a considered technical conversation between architecture and engineering. The details presented here are the project at its most precise.

The facade system is a pressure-equalised unitised curtain wall with integrated copper-alloy fins acting as brise-soleil. The connection between the tower core and the transfer structure at Level 4 is a bespoke steel link system engineered to handle differential thermal movement across a 120 m height.

Technical Details — Main Drawing

Facade Node

Unitised curtain wall. Pressure-equalised. U-value 0.8 W/m²K. Integrated copper fin brise-soleil at 400 mm centres.

Transfer Slab

200 mm PT flat slab over transfer beam grid at Level 4. 2,000 tonne transfer beam spans 18 m across atrium void.

Threshold

Continuous structural sill connecting ground-level plaza to riverbank promenade. Cast-in-place terrazzo finish.
