Architectural Design

Architecture

The architecture of Altan Nexus is structured around three interlocking moves: the podium, the tower, and the atrium. Each is architecturally and programmatically distinct yet inseparable as a spatial system.

The podium rises four floors from the riverbank promenade, containing retail, cultural, and civic programmes within a series of colonnaded halls and double-height galleries. The tower above contains thirty-four floors of residential and hospitality uses, set back from the podium edge to frame the atrium below.

Architecture — Main Drawing

Podium

Four-storey base containing retail, gallery, and public hall. Clad in precast concrete panels and recycled brick.

Tower

Thirty-four storeys of residential and sky-hotel. Articulated glass and copper panel facade with projecting brise-soleil.

Atrium

A 38-metre high naturally ventilated void at the heart of the building. The social and environmental centre of the project.
