Photorealistic Visualisation

Renders

The renders present Altan Nexus in full materiality — capturing the quality of light on the copper facade at dusk, the reflections of the Vardar in the atrium glazing, and the animated life of the riverfront promenade at night.

Rendered using V-Ray and Chaos Cloud. All exterior renders shot from surveyed camera positions to provide accurate massing representation within the existing cityscape. Interior renders use calibrated IES light data from specified luminaires.

Renders — Main Drawing

Exterior

Eight key viewpoints from the river, boulevard, and adjacent urban fabric. Dawn, dusk, and night conditions.

Interior

Atrium, sky lounge, residential lobby, and riverfront terrace. All rendered at 6K resolution.

Aerial

Drone-perspective aerials showing the building in the full Skopje city context and Vardar corridor.
